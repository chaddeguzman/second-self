# Brain Quarterly Review

Version: `1.0.0`

1. Summarize the prior quarter from reviews, decisions, projects, and lessons.
2. Compare current commitments with purpose, values, and strategy.
3. Surface contradictions and missing evidence.
4. Draft current-view changes without erasing historical sources.
5. Route every identity or strategy diff through both broker approvals.
6. Archive the review and regenerate indexes.
