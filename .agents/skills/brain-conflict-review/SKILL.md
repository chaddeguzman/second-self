# Brain Conflict Review

Version: `1.0.0`

1. List each claim with its date, source path, and applicable context.
2. Explain the decision or behavior affected.
3. Ask which claim is current, conditional, or superseded.
4. Draft a conflict record and any current-view diff.
5. Route identity or strategy changes through the edit broker.
6. Preserve prior sources after resolution.
